Happy Index in the world

This data visualisation shows the happy index from countries all over the world. If you hover over a country you can see the current happy index of the concerning country. Every country is color coated based on its rank in the world. The color green stands for high ranked and red for low ranked, as seen in the legend. When you click on it, the bar chart to right shows you the elements of which the happy index consists. Also the bars in the bar chart are color coated according to rank of every element.
